Go Live and Launch Assistance

During the development, intermediate versions of the product can be uploaded to the production server for demo purposes.

The final version of the product is uploaded according to the schedule, and the mobile versions are registered in the app stores. We help our clients to choose a domain name, hosting and cloud storage services.

Support and Troubleshooting

We stay in touch with our clients after the project handover to answer any questions a client may have, show how the app works, and help with any technical difficulties that may arise.

New Functionality Implementation

Taking into account all the user stories and client feedback, our project manager, and the team evaluate all the features that are not included in the current version of the product. After that, we can implement them in the next version of the product, using the modern development methodology and the most recent frameworks. It makes further support and development easier.
